# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone, regardless of
experience level, background, or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that helps:

- Being kind, respectful, and constructive.
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started.
- Accepting feedback gracefully and focusing on what's best for the project.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainer at
**vchapandrews@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate this
Code of Conduct, and may ban repeat offenders.
